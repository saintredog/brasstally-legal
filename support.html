Support

Brasstally logs your business miles automatically and works out your mileage deduction. If something is not behaving, start here. Most questions come down to one of the first three answers.

Drives are not being detected

Almost always location permission. Automatic detection needs Always, not "While Using the App", because the whole point is that it works with the app closed.

  • Open iPhone Settings > Brasstally > Location and choose Always.
  • In Brasstally, open Settings. The dot next to Location is green when it is set correctly and red when it is not.
  • Make sure Detect & record drives automatically is switched on.
  • Low Power Mode can delay the wake-up, so drives may start recording a little later than usual.

To check the recorder without waiting for a real trip, tap Start trip recording on the Drives tab and drive around the block. Same engine, triggered by hand.

The mileage looks low, or a short trip is missing

Trips under 0.3 miles are ignored on purpose, and the app waits for about 250 metres at driving speed before it commits to recording. That keeps walking and parking-lot manoeuvres out of your log. If a drive starts underground, recording begins at the first GPS fix, which is usually within a block of the true start. You can correct any drive by tapping it and editing the distance.

A trip was split in two, or ended early

A drive ends after about five minutes stopped. Brasstally tries to tell a traffic jam apart from a real destination and will hold a trip open much longer when it can see you creeping forward. If a trip still gets split, edit one and delete the other, or add a single replacement from the Add tab.

Moving data in from another app

Settings > Import data accepts JSON and CSV.

  • Coming from the Brasstally web app, use its Export all data (JSON). That is the lossless one and carries drives, vehicles, saved places, clients and your rate.
  • CSV from other mileage apps works too. Column names are matched loosely; it needs a date column and a miles column.
  • Merge keeps what is already on your phone and skips duplicates. Replace overwrites everything.

Backing up

Your data lives only on your phone, so nothing is backed up to us. Use Settings > Export backup (JSON) now and then and keep the file somewhere safe. It is the only copy that survives deleting the app.

Pro subscription

What Pro adds

  • Rules that classify drives automatically as they are detected
  • The actual-expense method, compared against standard mileage
  • A CPA-ready IRS tax packet PDF
  • Unlimited vehicles, each with its own expenses and deduction

Restoring a purchase

On a new phone, or after reinstalling, open the Pro screen and tap Restore purchase.

Cancelling

Pro is $34.99 per year and renews until cancelled. Cancel any time in iPhone Settings > your name > Subscriptions. Everything you have already recorded stays yours, and CSV export keeps working after Pro ends.

Is the deduction figure tax advice?

No. Brasstally does the arithmetic from the miles you record using the IRS standard rate for that year, and can compare it against the actual-expense method. It is a record-keeping tool, not a tax professional. Check your return with an accountant. IRS Publication 463 covers vehicle deductions.
