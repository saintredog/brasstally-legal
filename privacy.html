Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Brasstally keeps everything on your phone. There are no servers, no account, and no analytics. The developer never receives your drives, your locations, or any personal information.

What we collect

Nothing is sent to us. Brasstally has no backend and no user accounts. Your drives, vehicles, expenses, saved places, rules, and settings are stored only in the app's private storage on your device. We have no way to see them.

We do not use analytics, advertising, third-party trackers, or any software development kit that reports your activity to us or anyone else.

Location

With your permission, Brasstally uses your device's location to detect and record drives and to fill in start and end addresses. This location data is processed on your device and stored on your device. It is never transmitted to us.

To turn coordinates into a street address and to calculate driving distance, the app uses Apple's Maps services (MapKit and CLGeocoder), which are built into iOS. Those requests are handled by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive them.

You can change or revoke location access at any time in Settings → Brasstally → Location.

Purchases

The optional Pro subscription is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Apple handles payment; we never see your card number or billing details. Whether you have an active subscription is verified on your device through Apple's StoreKit. You can manage or cancel the subscription in your Apple account.

Your data and your control

  • All data stays on your device unless you export it.
  • Backups and reports you export (JSON, CSV, PDF) go only where you send them. The app never uploads them anywhere.
  • You can delete all data at any time in Settings → Clear all data, or by deleting the app.

Children

Brasstally is a tool for tracking business mileage and is not directed to children.

Changes to this policy
